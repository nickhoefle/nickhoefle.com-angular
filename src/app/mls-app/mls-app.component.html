MLStats

Developing this application has served as a way for me to get my soccer fix in between St. Louis CITY SC matches. Going beyond the pitch and into the numbers, this this project includes a homepage spotlighting league leaders, a player search, stat graphs, and a side-by-side player comparison function. Clicking team logos at the top takes you to individual franchise hubs with rosters, statistics, and charts. Behind the scenes statistics are stored across multiple JSON files.
