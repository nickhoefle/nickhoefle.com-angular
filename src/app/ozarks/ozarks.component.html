Ozarks GIS

I am a real estate junkie and decided to create this map to keep my GIS software skills sharp. Developed with QGIS, deployed with OpenLayers, and customized with JavaScript/CSS/HTML, this webmap can be used to track waterfront property at luxurious Lake Ozark, Missouri. The project utilizes OpenStreetMap for a base layer, latitude/longitude points for restaurants and areas of interest, and shapefiles for population data.
