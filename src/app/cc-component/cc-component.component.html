Collector's Corner

A movie, book, and board game connoisseur's dream! Collector's Corner started as a capstone project for LaunchCode's LiftOff program. I have enjoyed continuing to refine, expand, and introduce new features to this platform with my pal Rich.

Register, create your first book/movie/game collection, and you're off! Each medium has a search function to find favorites and add them to personalized lists for archiving or sharing with friends. Collections from all users are centralized in a page where users can explore what others have created and discover new favorites. Personalized SideNotes provide an opportunity to explain why a particular film belongs to a collection or express your love (or dislike) for it.
