MediaMosaic

Create a personalized news feed from 36 (and counting!) news outlets. MediaMosaic implements Google's Firebase for authentication and back-end services. Create an account with email, or log in with your Google account, pick your trusted news sources, select the amount of articles rendered per source, and you're set. When fresh articles are pushed to the XML feeds of your chosen press agencies, your feed is updated.
